About

Why it exists

This library came about because the glassmorphism CSS I found online didn't look anything like real glass. So I experimented with a bunch of freshly supported CSS to develop a more refined and three-dimensional glass look.

How it differs

from regular glassmorphism

Key characteristics

of next-gen glassmorphism

  • No white outlines!
  • Specular highlights
  • Fine-grained noise textures
  • Large blur radius
  • Bevels that fade smoothly into non-existence
